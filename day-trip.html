Overview

Escape the ordinary and embark on a private sailing adventure through the breathtaking Brač archipelago. This full-day experience takes you through crystal-clear Adriatic waters, hidden coves, and stunning coastal scenery that can only be accessed by boat.

Departing from the historic town of Omiš, you'll sail aboard the beautiful 54-foot yacht "Lučica" with experienced skipper Zdravko as your guide. Discover secluded swimming spots, snorkel in pristine waters, and enjoy the authentic Dalmatian sailing experience away from crowded tourist beaches.

What to Expect

09:00

Departure from Omiš

Meet at Omiš marina and board the sailing yacht Lučica. Safety briefing and welcome aboard.

09:30

Set Sail

Raise the sails and head out into the Adriatic. Enjoy the spectacular views of the Dalmatian coast and the Cetina River canyon.

11:00

First Swimming Stop

Anchor in a secluded bay for swimming and snorkeling. Crystal clear waters perfect for exploring underwater life.

13:00

Lunch Break

Optional stop at a coastal restaurant or enjoy a picnic on board (bring your own or pre-arrange catering).

14:30

Explore Brač Island Coast

Sail along the stunning northern coast of Brač island, discovering hidden beaches and dramatic cliffs.

15:30

Return Journey

Begin the relaxing sail back to Omiš, with time for one more swim if conditions permit.

17:00

Arrival

Return to Omiš marina with unforgettable memories of your Adriatic adventure.

Times are approximate and may vary based on weather conditions and group preferences. The itinerary can be customized to your wishes.
