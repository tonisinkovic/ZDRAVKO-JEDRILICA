Overview

Spend two unforgettable days sailing through the Omis Riviera and Brac archipelago on the private 54-foot yacht Lučica. This experience gives you more time to truly explore hidden bays, quiet coves, and iconic coastal scenery.

With skipper Zdravko, you will enjoy a relaxed pace, custom route options, and sunset moments at sea that are not possible on a standard day trip. Ideal for couples, families, and small private groups looking for a premium Adriatic adventure.

2-Day Itinerary

Day 1

Departure from Omis and Riviera exploration

Morning departure, scenic sailing, swimming stops, and optional lunch by the sea.

Sunset

Golden hour sailing and overnight stop

Enjoy sunset views, calm anchorage, and evening relaxation in a peaceful bay.

Day 2

Morning swim, sailing around Brac, final stop for snorkeling, and return in late afternoon.
